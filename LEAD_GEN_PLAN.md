# Pitbull Security – Call Volume Improvement Plan (York Region + GTA)

## Important reality check
No public source can verify a competitor's exact daily call count unless they publish analytics. Treat "they get so many calls" as a signal, not a measurable fact.

## What is publicly observable from Viewtech
- Broad market messaging (residential + commercial + multiple verticals).
- Strong and repeated "Get a Quote / Free Consultation" CTAs.
- Large visible testimonial/review section and social-proof emphasis.
- Geography breadth shown directly on their website and project pages.
- Dedicated "Our Work" section for proof and examples.

## Why this usually creates more inbound calls
1. **More indexed landing pages** = more long-tail search traffic.
2. **Clear CTA repetition** above the fold and throughout pages.
3. **Trust stack** (reviews + project examples + years in business) lowers hesitation.
4. **Fast response positioning** converts chats/forms into calls.
5. **Coverage clarity** helps visitors quickly self-qualify.

## 30-day execution plan for Pitbull

### Week 1: Conversion fundamentals
- Add sticky mobile call CTA and click-to-call button above the fold.
- Add a short "Get quote in 20 minutes" message if operationally true.
- Track all call clicks in GA4 + Search Console.

### Week 2: Local SEO depth
- Build city pages for: Vaughan, Markham, Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton.
- Add project-type pages: Office Access Control, Retail Alarm Upgrades, Warehouse Surveillance.
- Internal-link every service page to one city page + one project page.

### Week 3: Trust + proof
- Add 8-12 real project snippets with photos and short outcomes.
- Add FAQ schema and LocalBusiness schema.
- Add "before/after" security outcomes where possible.

### Week 4: Lead response + paid support
- Ensure first-response SLA under 10 minutes in business hours.
- Run narrow Google Ads campaigns on highest-intent terms only.
- Retarget site visitors with a simple quote/consultation offer.

## KPI targets (first 90 days)
- +40% click-to-call events.
- +30% form submissions.
- +25% organic service-page sessions.
- <10 minute median first response.

## Tracking checklist
- GA4 events: `click_call`, `click_email`, `form_submit`, `chat_start`, `chat_escalate`.
- GSC monitor for terms containing: access control, alarm system, CCTV, intercom + city names.
- Monthly dashboard by service + city page.
