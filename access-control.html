Access Control FAQs

Can we keep our existing doors and hardware?

Usually yes. We assess your current frame/door/hardware condition and recommend where to reuse, upgrade, or replace for reliability.

Do you support phased upgrades?

Absolutely. Many clients start with priority doors, then scale by floor, department, or building as budgets and timelines allow.

Can access events be tied to camera footage?

Yes. We can align access events with camera coverage to speed up verification and incident investigations.

How fast can we get a quote?

After a site review and scope call, we provide a clear proposal with options based on your operational requirements.
