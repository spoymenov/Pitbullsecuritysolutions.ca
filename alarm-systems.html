Alarm System FAQs

Can we keep our existing sensors?

In many cases yes. We evaluate wiring, sensor health, and panel compatibility before recommending replacements.

Can alarms and cameras work together?

Yes. Alarm events can trigger faster camera review workflows and improve event verification.

Do you support multi-tenant or partitioned setups?

Yes. We regularly build partitioned systems for buildings with different teams, units, or operating hours.

What determines alarm system pricing?

Property layout, zone count, communication requirements, integration scope, and response workflow complexity.
